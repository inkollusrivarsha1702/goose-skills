FOUNDER BRIEF · 2026

5 Tips for Building
a Startup in 20261

01

Start before you're ready

Waiting for perfect conditions is the slowest path to zero. The market rewards motion, not preparation.

02

Talk to ten customers a week

The market will tell you what to build. You just have to listen — and resist the urge to defend.

03

Narrow the problem relentlessly

The best products do one thing extraordinarily well. Cut features until the remaining ones feel inevitable.

04

Burn rate is the enemy of time

Runway is your most precious resource. Protect it fiercely — every dollar bought you a chance to be right tomorrow.

05

Hire character, teach skill

The first ten hires define the culture you'll spend a decade living inside. Choose for what you can't change.

1) Compiled from a year of founder interviews, post-mortems, and notes-to-self by builders who shipped, pivoted, and survived the current cycle. The order is not the priority — every tip is non-negotiable.
