• The five tips, in order.

01

Ship before you're ready.

Your users will tell you what's broken faster than any spec doc. Embarrass yourself early — it's cheaper than perfecting in private.

02

Distribution beats product.

A mediocre product with great reach wins every time. Build the channel as deliberately as you build the code.

03

Hire for execution, not credentials.

The best early hires ship things — not decks. Look for people who finish, not just people who can talk about finishing.

04

Default to async.

Every sync hour is expensive when time compounds. Write things down, push decisions to docs, and protect deep work.

05

Revenue is the only metric that matters.

In year one, everything else is a leading indicator at best. Charge people money — and let the bank account tell you the truth.
