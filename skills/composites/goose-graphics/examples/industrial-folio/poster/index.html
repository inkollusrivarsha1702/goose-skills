5 Tips for Building
a Startup in 2026

Five operating principles for founders shipping in a market where AI compounds advantage, capital is patient, and distribution beats novelty.

Ship Fast

Velocity is the only durable moat. Compress your cycle until shipping daily feels boring, then go faster.

AI in the Core

Build AI into the spine of the product, not the surface. The companies that win in 2026 retrained at the foundation.

Hire for Leverage

Headcount is a liability. Pick operators who clear ten meters of work for every meter of payroll.

Obsess Over Ten

Ten loud, loyal users will outpredict ten thousand quiet ones. Get on the phone, watch the screen, ship the fix.

Revenue Beats Runway

A dollar of revenue is a vote. A dollar of runway is a deadline. Optimise for the vote.
