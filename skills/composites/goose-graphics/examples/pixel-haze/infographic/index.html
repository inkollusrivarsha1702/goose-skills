FIELD GUIDE 2026

5 Tips for Building a
Startup in 2026.

A short field guide for founders who would rather move than meet. Five rules, learned the expensive way.

TIP 01

Ship fast, learn faster.

Iteration speed is the new competitive moat. Every week you don't ship, someone else does. Build the smallest thing that teaches you something real, then do it again.

TIP 02

Build AI into the core.

Don't bolt models on at the edges. Treat AI as day-one infrastructure — the spine your product reaches around, not the garnish on top of it.

TIP 03

Hire for leverage, not headcount.

One ten-x hire beats three average ones. Build a team where every additional person multiplies what the rest can do — and stay small until that's true.

TIP 04

Obsess over 10 users before 10,000.

Ten people who would be devastated if you disappeared beats ten thousand who'd shrug. Depth before scale, every time.

TIP 05

Revenue beats runway.

Charge people money. Paid users tell the truth — every dollar collected is a vote that what you built is worth more than they paid for it.
