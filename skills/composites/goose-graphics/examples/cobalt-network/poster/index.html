01
Ship fast, learn faster

Iteration speed beats planning. Embarrass yourself early; the market will correct your taste.

02
Build AI into the core

In 2026, AI is infrastructure. Embed it from day one — retrofitting later costs 3x more.

03
Hire for leverage, not headcount

A small team with sharp tooling outperforms a bloated org. One 10x hire beats three average.

04
Obsess over 10 users before 10,000

Deep retention with a few beats shallow acquisition at scale. Talk to users daily.

05
Revenue beats runway

Default to revenue-positive as early as possible. Fundraising is a tool, not a strategy.
