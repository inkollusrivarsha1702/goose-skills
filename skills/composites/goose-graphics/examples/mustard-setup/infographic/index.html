5 Tips for Building
a Startup in 2026

Everything you need to start. A friendly checklist for the first 90 days — what to ship, who to hire, how to think about runway, and why ten users beat ten thousand on day one.

01

Ship fast, learn faster

Iteration speed is the moat. Every week you don't ship is a week your competitor learns more about your shared user.

02

Build AI into the core

Not a feature on the side — the spine of the product. The core loop has to require the model, or someone else will rebuild you with one that does.

03

Hire for leverage, not headcount

Headcount is a liability — one operator with taste, judgment, and shipping speed beats five who require a process to function.

04

Obsess over 10 users before 10,000

Make ten people love it before you reach ten thousand who tolerate it. Lukewarm scale is the slowest way to die in 2026.

05

Revenue beats runway

A paying customer is the only proof anything is real. Everything else — including the round you raised last quarter — is a story you tell yourself.
