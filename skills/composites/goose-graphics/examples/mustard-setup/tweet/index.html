5 tips for building a startup in 2026:
1.Ship fast, learn faster. Every week you don't ship, your competitor learns more about your shared user.
2.Build AI into the core, not as a side feature. The product loop has to need the model.
3.Hire for leverage, not headcount. One operator with taste beats five with titles.
4.Obsess over 10 users before 10,000. Love before scale — lukewarm scale is a slow death.
5.Revenue beats runway. A paying customer is the only proof anything is real.
Save this for the moment you start over-planning instead of shipping.
