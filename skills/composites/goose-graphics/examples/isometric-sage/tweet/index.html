5 things I wish I knew before building my startup in 2026:
1 / AI agents are your first hire, not a tool. Onboard them like a team member. Give context, not just commands.
2 / Distribution beats product at seed stage. Nobody cares how good it is if nobody knows it exists.
3 / Build in public from day one. Your audience is your moat. Reputation compounds faster than revenue.
4 / Extend runway obsessively. Cash in the bank beats growth metrics until you hit $1M ARR.
5 / Be curious about users, not competitors. Copy their language. Ignore competitor noise.
