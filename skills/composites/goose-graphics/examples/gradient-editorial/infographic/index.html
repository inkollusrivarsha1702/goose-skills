5 Tips for Building a Startup in 2026

Distilled from operators who shipped, pivoted, and survived the AI wave. Five rules that travel from idea to scale.

5

TIP 01

Ship fast, learn faster

Launch an MVP in weeks, not months. Iteration speed beats planning every time. Embarrass yourself early — the market tells the truth your deck never will.

01

TIP 02

Build AI into the core

In 2026, AI is infrastructure, not a feature. Embed it from day one. Retrofitting later costs three times more in time and technical debt — pay the cost up front.

02

TIP 03

Hire for leverage, not headcount

Small teams with high-leverage tooling consistently outperform large bloated orgs. One 10× hire beats three average ones — and is cheaper to manage.

03

TIP 04

Obsess over 10 users before 10,000

Deep retention with a few beats shallow acquisition at scale. Talk to your users daily until you can’t stand not to. Depth compounds — funnels leak.

04

TIP 05

Revenue beats runway

Default to revenue-positive as early as possible. Fundraising is a tool, not a strategy. Charge people money — paid users tell you the truth in a way no investor ever will.
