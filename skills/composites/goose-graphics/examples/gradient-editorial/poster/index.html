5 Tips for Building a Startup in 2026

Distilled from operators who shipped, pivoted, and survived the current AI wave — five rules that travel well from idea to scale.

01

Ship fast, learn faster

Launch in weeks; iteration speed beats planning every time.

02

Build AI into the core

In 2026, AI is infrastructure. Bake it in from day one — retrofitting later costs 3× more.

03

Hire for leverage, not headcount

Small teams with sharp tools beat bloated orgs. One 10× hire beats three average ones.

04

Obsess over 10 users before 10,000

Deep retention with a few beats shallow acquisition at scale. Talk to users daily.

05

Revenue beats runway

Default revenue-positive early. Fundraising is a tool, not a strategy.

Five rules, distilled. Print this; pin it next to your monitor; run an honest audit on your current sprint against each one.
