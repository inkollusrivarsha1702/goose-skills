01
Ship fast, learn faster.
Iteration speed is the new moat. Every Friday without a release is a Friday a competitor caught up. Cut scope, not cycles.
Ship fast
02
Build AI into the core.
AI as a feature is already legacy. The startups that compound in 2026 are AI-native — the model is in the loop, not bolted on top.
AI core
03
Hire for leverage, not headcount.
Five people with great tools beat fifteen with a process. Optimize for output per person, not org-chart heft.
Leverage
04
Obsess over 10 users before 10,000.
Depth before breadth. Ten users who would cry if you shut down beat ten thousand who'd shrug. Earn intensity first.
10 users
05
Revenue beats runway.
Cheap capital is gone. The founders who win 2026 charge early, charge confidently, and let revenue — not a deck — extend their runway.
Revenue
