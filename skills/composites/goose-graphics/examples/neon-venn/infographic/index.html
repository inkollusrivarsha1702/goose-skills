2026 Playbook

5 Tips for
Building a Startup.

A short read on what shipped — five moves the operators of 2026 keep coming back to. Stack of pills, hairline structure, no decoration.

01

Velocity

Ship fast, learn faster

Ship fast, learn faster.

Median time-to-launch among the founders who broke out: seven days. Iteration speed beats perfect planning — embarrass yourself early.

02

Leverage

Build AI into the core

Build AI into the core.

Eighty-four percent of breakout startups in 2026 treat AI as infrastructure from day one. Retrofitting later costs ~3× in time and tech debt.

03

Leverage

Hire for leverage

Hire for leverage,
not headcount.

Small teams with high-leverage tooling outperform bloated orgs. One ten-times hire beats five average ones — and is dramatically easier to manage.

04

Discipline

Obsess over 10 users

Obsess over 10 users
before 10,000.

Deep retention with a small cohort outperforms shallow acquisition at scale. Talk to your first ten users daily until you can't stand not to.

05

Discipline

Revenue beats runway

Revenue beats runway.

The median breakout reaches $5M in ARR before raising a Series A. Default to revenue-positive early — fundraising is a tool, not a strategy.
